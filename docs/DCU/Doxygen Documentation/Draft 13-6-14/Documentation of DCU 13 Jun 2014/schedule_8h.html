enum SCH_STATUS

brief Enum to represent different status of the a schedule.

Enumerator
SCH_STATUS_ALWAYS_ACTIVE 

Applicable for the schedules in which we have to control DO based on status of DI. There is not dependency on the date and time.

SCH_STATUS_EXPIRES 

A schedule expires when the current date is greater than the end date. We have to ignore all the DI events.

SCH_STATUS_ACTIVE 

A schedule is active when the current date is the schedule date and the DI is been detected and correspodning changes in DO has taken place.

SCH_STATUS_NOT_ACTIVE 

A schedule is not active when the current date is the schedule date and DI is not been detected and current time is not in schedule start and end time.

SCH_STATUS_NOT_ELIGIBLE_TODAY 

A schedule is not eligible today when the current date is not the schedule date. We have to ignore all the DI events.

SCH_STATUS_IN_PROGRESS 

A schedule is in process in case of simple schedule.
